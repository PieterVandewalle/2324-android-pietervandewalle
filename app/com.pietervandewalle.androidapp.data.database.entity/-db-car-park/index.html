Represents an entity in the database for storing car park information.

Parameters

id

The unique identifier for the car park. Automatically generated by the database.

name

The name of the car park.

lastUpdate

The timestamp of the last update for car park information.

totalCapacity

The total capacity of the car park.

availableCapacity

The currently available capacity of the car park.

description

A description of the car park.

extraInfo

Extra information about the car park (optional).

isOpenNow

Indicates whether the car park is currently open.

isTemporaryClosed

Indicates whether the car park is temporarily closed.

isFree

Indicates whether the car park is free to use.

isInLEZ

Indicates whether the car park is located in a Low Emission Zone (LEZ).

operator

The operator of the car park.

location

The GPS coordinates of the car park location.
